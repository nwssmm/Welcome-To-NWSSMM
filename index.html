Welcome to NWSSMM

Northwest Student Chapter of the Society for Marine Mammalogy

Welcome to the Northwest Student Chapter of the Society for Marine Mammalogy (NWSSMM)! We are a community of graduate and undergraduate students primarily based in Oregon, Washington, and British Columbia, united by our shared interest in marine mammal science.

Who We Are

NWSSMM connects students across the Pacific Northwest who are passionate about marine mammals. While many of our members are affiliated with academic institutions, everyone is welcome to participate and contribute to our community.

  • Oregon State University
  • University of Washington
  • Western Washington University
  • University of British Columbia
  • University of Victoria

Our professional sponsor is Dr. Andrew Trites from the University of British Columbia and Dr. Amy Van Cise from the University of Washington.

What We Do

  • Annual Chapter Meeting: rotating yearly across PNW institutions. The 2025 meeting is TBD.
  • Biannual Newsletter: published in Fall and Spring.
  • Networking & Symposia: informal gatherings and research events.
